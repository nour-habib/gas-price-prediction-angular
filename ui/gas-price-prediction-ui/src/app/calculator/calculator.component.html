Answer

Calculate the gas price based on the USA consumer price index, crude oil price and oil production (per barrel). The formula is based on a polynomial regression model using data obtained from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics.


Gas prices are influenced by many factors including geopolitical and economical. This model intends to predict the average price of regular unleaded gas per litre (CAD) in the United States. For more details about the model, see the Model section.
